Welcome to the complete beginner's guide to technical writing. If you're looking to break into the field of technical writing or need a comprehensive guide to the world of technical writing, you're at the right place.

Technical writing is a job that can be rewarding and challenging at the same time. It requires you to have a good understanding of the subject matter and it also requires you to be able to explain it in simple terms.

If you are looking to take on this career, there are certain steps that you need to follow:

Acquire a relevant degree or diploma in technical communication. This is optional.

Gain experience in writing in your field of interest.

Learn how to communicate effectively through written mediums.
